For Boat Operators & Skippers

Your vessel. Your route. Your terms.

Oceanid gives professional skippers and fleet operators the tools to publish passages, fill seats, and coordinate every stop, without the spreadsheets.

edit_calendar

Publish your voyage

Create a passage with stops, dates, and per-leg pricing. Set it live in minutes. Oceanid handles discovery, bookings, and passenger communications.

groups

Manage your manifest

Review join requests, approve passengers, and keep your manifest up to date. All crew documents and certifications in one encrypted vault.

local_gas_station

Coordinate marina services

Attach docking, high-flow fueling, and provisioning directly to each stop. Request services in one tap, confirmed before you arrive.

payments
account_balance

Track commercial performance

Monitor revenue per passage, seat occupancy, and booking trends. From a single yacht to a commercial fleet. Full financial visibility in one dashboard.

For Travelers & Guests

Experience the passage, not the paperwork.

Find an open voyage, pick your stop, request to join. Your skipper approves. Your boarding documents arrive in the app.

2

3
